**Layer:** Derivatives · **Primitive:** Encrypted Margin + Positions

Full-featured perpetual futures where position sizes, leverage, PnL, and funding rates are all encrypted on-chain.

## What this means

- Open, manage, and close leveraged positions with no on-chain footprint visible to others.
- Margin accounts are encrypted fhERC-20 balances — collateral is hidden.
- Funding rate settlement happens over encrypted aggregate data — your funding exposure is private.
- Liquidations are triggered by encrypted health factors: keepers see only **"position underwater: yes / no"** — never the size, entry price, or strategy.

## What's hidden

- Position direction (long / short)
- Position size and leverage
- Entry and liquidation price
- Accumulated PnL
- Margin balance

## Why it matters

On public perp platforms, large positions are visible. Liquidation levels are known. Whale positions get hunted. With private perps, nobody knows where your stop is — or that you're even in a trade.
