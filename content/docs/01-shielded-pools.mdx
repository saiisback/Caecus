**Layer:** Foundation · **Standard:** fhERC-20

Every asset in Caecus lives as an **fhERC-20 token** — an encrypted version of ERC-20 where balances are ciphertexts, not plaintext numbers.

## What this means

- Your token balance is encrypted on-chain — wallets show directional updates without revealing amounts.
- Pool reserves are real and deep — liquidity is consolidated, not fragmented across anonymous UTXOs.
- Deposits, withdrawals, and transfers all happen within the encrypted state — no amount is ever visible to block explorers, competitors, or bots.
- Anyone can provide liquidity; nobody can see how much you've provided.

## Why it matters

On transparent chains, whale wallets are tracked in real time. Competitors know your positions. Copy traders front-run your strategy. With shielded pools, your liquidity is invisible — you retain full informational advantage.
