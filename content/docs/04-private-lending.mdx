**Layer:** Credit · **Primitive:** Encrypted Borrow / Lend

A full lending protocol — think Aave — where every collateral position, borrow amount, interest rate, and health factor is encrypted.

## What this means

- Deposit collateral as encrypted fhERC-20 — nobody knows what you've posted or how much.
- Borrow against it without revealing your loan-to-value ratio.
- Interest accrues silently in encrypted state.
- Liquidation bots check health factors via CoFHE: they receive a boolean (**"liquidate: yes / no"**) — the position details stay sealed, even from the keeper.
- Institutions can borrow at scale without revealing strategy or counterparty exposure.

## What's hidden

- Collateral type and amount
- Borrow size and asset
- Health factor and liquidation threshold
- Interest owed

## Why it matters

Transparent lending protocols expose every institution's risk profile. Competitors can see if a whale is over-leveraged. Protocol-level front-running of liquidations is rampant. Private lending makes all of this impossible.
