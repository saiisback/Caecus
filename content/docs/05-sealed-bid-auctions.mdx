**Layer:** Institutional · **Primitive:** Encrypted Order Flow

A private order book and sealed-bid auction mechanism that sits alongside the AMM for large block trades and price discovery.

## What this means

- Institutional traders post large orders as encrypted intents — size and price are hidden until settlement.
- **Sealed-bid auctions:** all bids remain encrypted during the auction window; only the winner is revealed at close.
- **Dark pool matching:** orders match against each other inside CoFHE without either side learning the other's parameters until execution.
- NFT auctions, token launches, and protocol liquidations can all run through sealed bids.

## What's hidden

- Bid amounts and identities
- Order book depth and composition
- Block trade sizes pre-settlement

## Why it matters

Dark pools exist in TradFi precisely because large orders move markets when visible. For the first time, on-chain dark pool mechanics are possible — giving institutional participants a venue that doesn't telegraph their strategy.
