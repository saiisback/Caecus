**Layer:** Asset · **Primitive:** Confidential Real-World Assets

A wrapper layer for tokenizing real-world assets — treasuries, private credit, real estate — inside the shielded pool ecosystem.

## What this means

- RWA tokens are issued as fhERC-20 — holder identities and balances are encrypted.
- Institutional asset holders can bring real-world collateral on-chain without revealing counterparty relationships.
- Yield from RWAs accrues in encrypted state — earnings are private.
- Compatible with selective disclosure for regulatory compliance.

## Why it matters

J.P. Morgan has explored tokenizing $1.5T in AUM on-chain — but found that customer-level privacy was a hard requirement. Transparent blockchains can't meet that bar. Caecus can.
