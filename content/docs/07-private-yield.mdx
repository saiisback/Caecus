**Layer:** Yield · **Primitive:** Hidden Fee Accrual

LP positions and yield-bearing receipts are encrypted fhERC-20 tokens. Your yield farming activity is completely private.

## What this means

- LP receipt tokens are encrypted — nobody can see how much liquidity you've provided to any pool.
- Fee accrual happens in encrypted state — yield accumulation is invisible.
- Staking and unstaking don't reveal your position size.
- Yield strategies can be executed without signaling to copy traders or competitors.

## What's hidden

- LP position sizes across all pools
- Accumulated fees and yield
- Staking amounts and durations
- Yield strategy composition
