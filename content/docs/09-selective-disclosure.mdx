**Layer:** Compliance · **Primitive:** Institutional-Grade Transparency Controls

Every user can generate private viewing keys that selectively reveal their positions to specific parties — auditors, regulators, counterparties — without making anything public.

## What this means

- Generate a time-bounded viewing key for any position or account.
- Share with a regulator, compliance team, or counterparty — they see the plaintext; the chain still shows ciphertexts.
- Audit-ready without compromising on-chain privacy.
- Institutional participants can prove solvency or collateral to counterparties without public disclosure.

## Why it matters

This is the feature that makes private DeFi viable for institutions. They need to show positions to their compliance teams, auditors, and counterparties. Selective disclosure does this without breaking the privacy model for everyone else.
