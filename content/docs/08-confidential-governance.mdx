**Layer:** Governance · **Primitive:** Encrypted Voting

Protocol governance where votes, voter identities, and token weights can all remain private until (or after) a vote concludes.

## What this means

- Governance token balances are encrypted — voting power is hidden until you choose to reveal it.
- Votes are encrypted on submission — no one can see how anyone voted during the voting window.
- Results are decrypted and published at vote close — full verifiability, zero coercion.
- Whale wallets cannot coordinate visible voting blocs or apply social pressure on smaller holders.
- Works for: fee parameter changes, new pool listings, risk parameter updates, protocol upgrades.

## Why it matters

On-chain governance today is theatrics. Whales signal early, others follow. Private governance gives every token holder a genuine secret ballot — making outcomes reflect actual conviction, not social pressure.
