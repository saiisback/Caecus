**Layer:** AMM · **Built on:** Uniswap v4 Hooks + FHE

Private swaps plug into Uniswap v4's existing liquidity via FHE hooks. You don't give up deep liquidity for privacy — you get both.

## What this means

- Swap inputs, outputs, and token pairs are encrypted before hitting the mempool.
- The AMM curve runs on encrypted reserves — trade execution is verified without exposing trade size.
- Slippage parameters, paths, and token identities can all be hidden.
- LP fees accrue to encrypted LP receipt tokens — your earnings are private.

## What's hidden

- Who is swapping
- What they're swapping
- How much they're swapping
- At what price

## Why it matters

Every swap on a transparent AMM is a signal. MEV bots read your intent and extract value before your trade even settles. Private swaps make sandwich attacks cryptographically impossible — bots can't front-run what they can't see.
