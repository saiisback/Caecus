## What we're building

Caecus is a complete DeFi suite — pools, swaps, lending, perpetuals, governance — where **nothing is visible on-chain**. Not your balance. Not your position size. Not your strategy. Not your trades.

Every primitive you expect from DeFi, now with institutional-grade confidentiality baked in at the protocol level.

Caecus is not wrapping privacy on top of existing protocols. We are building privacy-first from the ground up on **Fhenix**, using **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)** — the only cryptographic approach that lets you compute directly on encrypted data without ever decrypting it.

## The core technology

**Fhenix CoFHE** (FHE Coprocessor) offloads the heavy cryptographic work off-chain while keeping verifiability on-chain. The result: **20,000× higher throughput** and **37× lower latency** compared to earlier FHE systems. Private DeFi at production speed.

- Data stays **encrypted during computation** — not just at rest or in transit.
- The chain sees only ciphertexts — **raw values are never exposed on-chain**.
- Smart contracts run math on encrypted numbers and return encrypted results.
- No new VM, no new language — **pure Solidity**, familiar tooling.
